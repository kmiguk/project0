Tokyo, Japan

Located in the North Pacific Ocean, there's a busy capital that mixes the ultramodern and the traditional, as well as having neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples. Very famous for its anime, video games, food and so much more! This is Tokyo.

Things To Do In Tokyo:

If you ever find yourself in Japan during the months of late March up to early May, definitely check out the Sakura Festival. When this is happening, Japan is covered in a soft pink splendor by Cherry Blossoms. It's a beautiful sight you shouldn't miss!
